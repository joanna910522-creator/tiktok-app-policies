Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 31, 2025

Key Point: This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information beyond what is required for TikTok API integration.

1. Application Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the "n8n TikTok Automation" application handles information in connection with automated video publishing to TikTok.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

This application does not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal identification information
  • User behavior data or analytics
  • Device information or location data
  • Contact information or communication records
  • Financial or payment information
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

3. Information We Access

The application only accesses information necessary for its function:

  • Video Files: From your Google Drive for publishing
  • TikTok API Access: To upload and publish videos to your account
  • File Names: To extract caption content for posts

4. How Information is Used

All accessed information is used solely for:

  • Retrieving video files from your Google Drive
  • Extracting captions from file names
  • Publishing content to your TikTok account
  • No other purposes whatsoever

5. Data Storage and Retention

The application operates on a "pass-through" basis:

  • No data is permanently stored on our servers
  • Video files are temporarily processed and immediately discarded
  • No logs or records of your content are maintained
  • API tokens are used only for authenticated requests

6. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:

  • TikTok API: Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • Google Drive API: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • n8n Platform: Subject to n8n's Privacy Policy

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All API communications use secure HTTPS protocols
  • No sensitive data is logged or stored
  • Access tokens are handled according to platform best practices

8. User Rights and Control

You maintain full control over your data:

  • You can revoke API access at any time through TikTok and Google settings
  • You can stop using the application without any data retention concerns
  • You control which videos are processed and published

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please note that this is a personal automation tool with minimal data handling.
